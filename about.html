About Me

Evan Eisenmenger is an aspiring photographer specializing in Nature, Macro, Landscape, and Portrait photography. Professionals have called him 'the closest a sixteen-year old can get to professional'. His images have received publicity from Nikon, gaining him exposure to thousands of individuals. When he isn't studying or taking photos, he enjoys playing soccer, spending time with his family, and relaxing. Evan currently lives in Palatine Illinois, and is a Junior at Palatine High School.
